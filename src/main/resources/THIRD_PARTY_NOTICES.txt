This mod distribution bundles a nested copy of Plasmo Voice for Fabric.

Bundled component:
- Plasmo Voice 2.1.8 for Fabric 1.21.11
- Source: https://github.com/plasmoapp/plasmo-voice
- Release asset: https://github.com/plasmoapp/plasmo-voice/releases/download/2.1.8/plasmovoice-fabric-1.21.11-2.1.8.jar
- License declared by upstream: LGPL-3.0

The bundled Plasmo Voice jar is loaded by Fabric Loader as a nested mod alongside
CosmicPrisonsMod. Upstream ownership and licensing remain with the Plasmo Voice authors.
